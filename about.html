About EcoDott

Our Story

EcoDott was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves to experience the joy and benefits of living with plants. Founded by plant enthusiasts who understand the transformative power of greenery, we've made it our mission to bring nature into every home.

What started as a small collection of rescued plants has grown into a curated selection of premium indoor plants, each chosen for their beauty, resilience, and ability to thrive in home environments.
